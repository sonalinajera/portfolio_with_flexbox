Greetings!

  1. I'm a transgender person of color and I use they/them pronouns.
  2. I enjoy working with queer non-profits to get their development needs met.
  3. I'm living the California Dream from San Francisco, but I am more than happy to work remotely for you!

While I enjoy primarily working with LGBTQ+ organizations these are not the only projects I've built out. If you're looking for a developer, then I hope you have found what you're looking for.

About Me

As a proud queer individual that has strong foundations in STEM. My journey began while attending UC Berkeley, where I got my degree in Molecular and Cell Biology. I worked in labs for a while but found the lack of community disheartening. I moved to San Francisco and made my way through the tech field. A lot of my early work was with education and tech, but I quickly realized that I needed to be on the learner side!

As I made my career a reality I started to see a huge gap in access to resources for queer organizations. Mainly, a lot of essential queer orgs had underdeveloped and uninspired web pages. I saw an opportunity to give back, improve, automate, and bring forward their presence. Insert Thinkful! With their help I launched my developer career and used my powers for the good of the people. I'm proud to know that I am able to give back.

When I'm not coding you can find me exploring the City's best views

I also do local orginzing and community outreach for my transgender resource group.

Finally, I spend time dancing Mexican Folklorico. One of my all time passion.
